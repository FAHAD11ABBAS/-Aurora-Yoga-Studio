Our Instructors

Meet the experienced teachers guiding your practice

Expert Guidance for Your Journey

Our certified instructors bring years of experience and passion to every class

Emma Larson

Lead Instructor & Studio Director

Emma is a certified yoga teacher with over 15 years of experience. She specializes in Vinyasa Flow and has trained extensively in India and Bali. Her classes focus on mindful movement and breath awareness.

Certifications: RYT-500, Meditation Teacher Training

Specialties: Vinyasa, Restorative, Meditation

Maya Chen

Senior Instructor

Maya brings warmth and compassion to her teaching. With a background in physical therapy, she offers a therapeutic approach to yoga, making it accessible for all bodies and abilities.

Certifications: RYT-200, Prenatal Yoga, Yin Yoga

Specialties: Gentle Yoga, Yin, Prenatal

Alex Rivera

Power & Ashtanga Instructor

Alex's dynamic classes challenge students to push beyond their limits while maintaining alignment and breath control. His energetic teaching style inspires strength and confidence.

Certifications: RYT-500, Ashtanga Certified

Specialties: Ashtanga, Power Yoga, Vinyasa

Sarah Williams

Meditation & Hatha Instructor

Sarah's practice emphasizes the spiritual aspects of yoga. Her meditation classes create a peaceful space for inner exploration, while her Hatha classes build a strong foundation.

Certifications: RYT-200, Advanced Meditation Training

Specialties: Hatha, Meditation, Mindfulness
