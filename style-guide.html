Aurora Yoga Studio

Brand Style Guide & Design System

Version 1.0 | December 2025

Introduction

Aurora Yoga Studio offers yoga classes, mindfulness practices, and wellness retreats. Our target audience is adults looking for balance and relaxation in everyday life. The brand aims to create a calm and trustworthy impression that encourages visitors to book classes and explore services.

Design Philosophy: Calm, minimalist, nature-inspired with clean fonts, airy layouts, and natural photography elements.

7. Website Structure

  • Home: Peaceful hero image, slogan, and CTA ("Book a Class")

  • Services: Yoga classes, retreats, mindfulness workshops

  • Contact: Email, phone, address, social media links, contact form
